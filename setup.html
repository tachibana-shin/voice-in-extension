Welcome!

Welcome to the Voice In setup page
Let's get you started with Voice In in 30 seconds. Click Next.

Step 1: Grant Microphone Access

Click on the Allow Microphone Access button below.
Make sure you have a microphone available on your system so you can dictate. An internal microphone is good enough.

When the prompt shows up, click Allow

Click Here to enable access and refresh the page

Step 2: Select Your Dictation Language

Don't worry, you can change this later.

Step 3: Pin Voice In Start/Stop Button

Click the at the top-right corner of your browser window (right of the search bar) and pin Voice In for easy access. Doing this will add the Voice In launch button (which has a microphone icon) to the address bar.

You can skip this step. Not applicate for Microsoft Edge.

Step 4: Try Voice In

Let's try Voice In now to make sure it is working correctly.
Click on the Voice In button at the top-right corner to start dictation (may take a few seconds, icon will turn red once started)
Place your cursor in the textbox below and say something e.g., "this works quite well".

Below is a quick video demoing the action. Click Next (button below) once you are done.

⚙️ Setup Complete

To use Voice In on a website, do the same as in the previous step.
1) Go to the website. Click on the Voice In button in the address bar to start dictation.
2) Place the cursor in the text box and start dictation :)
